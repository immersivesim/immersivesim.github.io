Study Name: Polygenic Risk Score Simulation, Patient-Provider Communication, and Interaction in a Virtual Reality Clinical Setting.

Principal Investigator: Susan Persky, PhD

Dear Prospective Study Participant,

A research team at the National Institutes of Health is inviting internal and family medical residents to participate in a study on telemedicine and clinical decision-making.

The study will be conducted remotely and will consist of a simulated telemedicine appointment to study risk communication and decision-making.

You may be eligible if:

  • You are a medical resident in a US-based internal or family medicine program.
  • You have access to a laptop or desktop computer with internet access, a working webcam, keyboard, and mouse with the ability to run Zoom during your participation (no tablets or phones).

Study sessions will be conducted over Zoom and last approximately 1 hour. Flexible scheduling provided.

Participants will receive a $100 Amazon gift card upon completion.
