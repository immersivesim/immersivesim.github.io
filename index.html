A research team at the National Institutes of Health is inviting internal and family medicine residents to participate in a study on telemedicine and clinical decision-making. The study will be conducted remotely and will consist of a simulated telemedicine appointment to study risk communication and decision-making.

You may be eligible if:

  • You are a medical resident in a US-based internal or family medicine program.

  • You have access to a laptop or desktop computer with internet access, a working webcam, keyboard, and mouse with the ability to run teleconferencing software during your participation (no tablets or phones).

Study sessions will be conducted online and lasts approximately 1 hour. Flexible scheduling provided.

Participants will receive a $100 Amazon gift card upon completion.
